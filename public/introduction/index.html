Overview

You will be using images like the one shown below to select clouds

You are aiming to select clouds near the tropopause. For those of you who don't know what that is, it is the boundary between 2 layers of the Earth's atmosphere the troposphere and the stratosphere. The tropopause is usually located around the bottom third of the image. In the image below, it is the solid gray line.
